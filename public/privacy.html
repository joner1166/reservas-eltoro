Privacy Policy

Il Toro E La Capra | 6435 S Decatur Blvd, Las Vegas, NV 89118 | Effective date: July 2026

1. Information We Collect

When you make a reservation or join our waitlist, we collect your first name, last name, phone number, email address (optional), party size, and any special requests you provide.

2. How We Use Your Information

We use your information exclusively to:

  • Confirm your reservation via SMS
  • Notify you when your table is ready (waitlist)
  • Manage your booking (modifications or cancellations)

3. SMS Messaging

SMS notifications are sent only to customers who explicitly opt in by checking the SMS consent checkbox on the reservation form. Providing your phone number alone does not constitute consent to receive SMS messages. Consent is not required to complete your reservation. Message and data rates may apply. Message frequency: up to 2 messages per reservation. Reply STOP at any time to opt out. Reply HELP for assistance.

4. Data Sharing

We do not sell, share, or disclose your personal information to third parties for marketing purposes. Your data is used solely for reservation management.

5. Data Retention

Reservation data is retained for operational and accounting purposes and deleted upon request.

6. Contact

For questions or data removal requests, contact us at Iltoroelacapra@toroelacapra.com.
