SMS Opt-In – Il Toro E La Capra

Il Toro E La Capra | 6435 S Decatur Blvd, Las Vegas, NV 89118 | SMS Reservation Notifications

How Customers Opt In

When a customer completes a reservation at toroelacapra.com, they are presented with the following opt-in checkbox before submitting their information. The checkbox is unchecked by default and is completely optional — customers can complete their reservation without checking it.

* This checkbox is unchecked by default and is completely optional. Customers can complete their reservation without providing SMS consent.

SMS Program Details

  • Sender: Il Toro E La Capra
  • Message types: Reservation confirmation, table-ready notification (waitlist)
  • Message frequency: Up to 2 messages per reservation
  • Opt-out: Reply STOP at any time
  • Help: Reply HELP or email Iltoroelacapra@toroelacapra.com
  • Rates: Msg & data rates may apply

Walk-In / Waitlist Opt-In

For walk-in guests who join the waitlist in person, the hostess verbally informs them that by providing their phone number they will receive up to 2 SMS notifications (waitlist confirmation + table-ready alert), and that they can reply STOP to opt out at any time.

Note: This consent notice is displayed directly in the hostess walk-in form at the reservation site above the "Add to Wait List" button, ensuring the hostess is reminded to communicate this to every walk-in guest before submitting.
