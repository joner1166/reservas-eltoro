SMS Opt-In – Il Toro E La Capra

Il Toro E La Capra | 6435 S Decatur Blvd, Las Vegas, NV 89118 | SMS Reservation Notifications

Il Toro E La Capra is a restaurant in Las Vegas, NV. This page documents the SMS program used by our reservation system at bookingeltoro.netlify.app. Messages are sent only to guests who make a reservation — online, by phone, or as an in-person walk-in — and consist exclusively of transactional notifications directly tied to that reservation (booking confirmation, waitlist confirmation, table-ready alert). No guest receives more than 2 messages, and we do not send marketing messages of any kind.

How Customers Opt In (Online Reservations)

When a customer completes a reservation at bookingeltoro.netlify.app, they are presented with the following opt-in checkbox before submitting their information. The checkbox is unchecked by default and is completely optional — customers can complete their reservation without checking it.

* This checkbox is unchecked by default and is completely optional. Customers can complete their reservation without providing SMS consent.

SMS Program Details

  • Sender: Il Toro E La Capra
  • Message types (3 total, transactional only — no marketing):
    1. Reservation Confirmation — sent once, immediately after a table reservation is booked online or by phone
    2. Waitlist Confirmation — sent once, immediately after an in-person walk-in guest is added to the waitlist
    3. Table-Ready Notification — sent once, when a waitlisted/walk-in guest's table becomes available
  • Message frequency: Up to 2 messages per guest (a booked reservation only ever triggers message #1; a walk-in only ever triggers messages #2 and #3) — never more, and never unrelated/marketing content
  • Opt-out: Reply STOP at any time
  • Help: Reply HELP or email Iltoroelacapra@toroelacapra.com
  • Rates: Msg & data rates may apply

How Customers Opt In (Phone & Walk-In Reservations)

For reservations taken by phone or in person (walk-in / waitlist), no web form is used, so consent is collected verbally by the hostess before the guest's phone number is entered into our system: the hostess tells the guest they will receive a text message related to their reservation (a confirmation for phone bookings, or a waitlist confirmation followed by a table-ready alert for walk-ins), that message and data rates may apply, and that they can reply STOP at any time to opt out. This same reminder is built into the hostess's booking screen, displayed directly above the submit button, so the hostess repeats it for every phone or walk-in guest before saving their number.
