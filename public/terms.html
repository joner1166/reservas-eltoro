Program Description

Il Toro E La Capra's SMS reservation program sends automated text messages to confirm reservations, provide waitlist updates, and notify guests when their table is ready.

Message Frequency

Message frequency varies. You will typically receive 1–3 messages per reservation (confirmation, updates, and/or table-ready notification).

Message & Data Rates

Message and data rates may apply depending on your mobile carrier and plan.

How to Opt Out

Reply STOP to any SMS message to unsubscribe from future messages. You will receive a final confirmation that you have been removed.
